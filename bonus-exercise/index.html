My Special Interest in Sketching

I have always had a love for drawing and Art. In particular, I have a passion for sketching people. Mostly I grab an photography portrait I am attracted to and see if I can capture it.


I don't know why, but I have always been attracted to faces that show emotion coming from within. The feeling's that arn't obvious. Like Sadness, and Longing. Or the quiet signs of a hard life lived. Ironically I am none of those things. Below is a a classic example of that kind of sketch.
